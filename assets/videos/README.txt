Put your hero render here, named exactly:

    hero.mp4

The homepage picks it up automatically and the placeholder disappears.

What works best:
  - 10 to 20 seconds long
  - No audio track (it is muted anyway, and it saves file size)
  - 1920x1080, H.264
  - Under about 10 MB, or the page will feel slow to load
  - A slow turntable spin of one assembly. The scroll scrubs through the
    frames, so a steady rotation reads as the model spinning under your thumb.

Export from KeyShot, SolidWorks Visualize, Fusion, or Blender.
